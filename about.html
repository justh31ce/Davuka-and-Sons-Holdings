About Davuka & Sons

Company Overview

Davuka & Sons Holding (Pty) Ltd is a 100% Black-Owned South African company established in 2016. We provide professional Electrical, Mechanical, Plumbing, Civil Engineering, Construction and Project Management services across multiple sectors.

Our vision is to become a leading engineering and infrastructure partner through quality workmanship, innovation and sustainable business practices.

Vision

To be a sustainable provider of excellent engineering and construction solutions while continuously supporting our clients.

Mission

To deliver quality services through continuous improvement, customer involvement and professional excellence.
